Custom Graphics

April 30th, 2022

Today the goal was to produce some custom graphics that are completely my own so as to avoid any possible copyright infringement issues. So far I successfully produced a soccer ball, both a regular one and one with a firetrailing behing it, a soccer jersey that is based of a real one's actual dimensions with a few changes, and the cleat image. The hardest one was the cleat due to the toughness to get actual dimensions and it was purely based on eyeballing. The jersey was actually almost as hard because it is so hard to measure the actual dimensions and then match the exact curves of the jersey without a protractor or anything like it to measure the angles of the different parts of the jersey. Eight more images are required to be selected and designed over the course of the next few weeks. The current images and graphics may not be the same as what my custom graphics are.
